About Truth Lens

AI-Powered Fake News Detection System

Our Mission

Truth Lens is a machine learning-based web application that helps identify fake news using advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques. Built with BERT, Django, and Bootstrap, our goal is to empower users to verify the authenticity of articles and URLs in real time.

High Accuracy

Utilizes BERT with over 90% fake news classification accuracy.

Real-Time

Provides instant analysis of both URLs and article text.

Source Validation

Compares information against verified databases and sources.

Explainable AI

Shows how decisions are made through interpretable results.

Technology Stack

  • Python, Django, Bootstrap 5
  • Transformers: BERT model
  • BeautifulSoup, lxml for scraping
  • JavaScript for interactivity
